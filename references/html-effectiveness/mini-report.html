Renewable Energy Adoption in APAC Markets

Across the Asia-Pacific region, renewable energy capacity grew by 23% year-over-year, driven primarily by solar installations in China and India. Government subsidies and declining panel costs accelerated deployment beyond initial projections.

However, grid integration remains a bottleneck. Several markets reported curtailment rates above 8%, indicating that transmission infrastructure has not kept pace with generation capacity.

Key Takeaway

Grid modernization investment must double by 2028 to prevent curtailment from eroding the economic gains of renewable deployment in high-growth APAC markets.
